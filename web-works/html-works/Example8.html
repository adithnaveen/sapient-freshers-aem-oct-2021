Publicis Sapient is an American digital consulting company, founded as Sapient in Cambridge, Massachusetts in 1990 by Jerry Greenberg and J. Stuart Moore.[2][3] On February 6, 2015, Sapient became a fully owned division of French advertising giant Publicis, in a deal valued at $3.7bn (£2.3bn).[4] On November 17, 2016, Publicis Sapient announced SapientNitro was merging with sister agency Razorfish to form SapientRazorfish, which would be led by former SapientNitro CEO Alan Wexler. Publicis.
